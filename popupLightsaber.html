SmartCane

The purpose of this project was to create a cane-like device that could detect two sinusoidal signals in wires buried underground, using only amplifiers and discrete circuit elements (no programming). When the wires are placed along a sidewalk, this cane could be used to sense the edges of the sidewalk and lead a (presumably blind) user along the path. We added a special touch to the SmartCane by using a toy Lightsaber for the body of the cane. Although we inserted our own circuitry into the handle, we preserved the Lightsaber's original ability to light up! An inductor in the tip of the Lightsaber senses the underground signals, and two potentiometers control the left and right headphones to ear the signals.
